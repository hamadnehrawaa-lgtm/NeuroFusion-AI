
 NeuroFusion AI

## Predicting Human Health Before Symptoms Appear

NeuroFusion AI is a predictive healthcare platform that combines:

- Smartwatch biosignals
- Smartphone behavioral biomarkers
- Genetic and family-health history
- Adaptive Artificial Intelligence

### Repository Contents

- app.py → Predictive AI prototype
- prototype.py → Risk prediction model
- requirements.txt → Project dependencies
- Website Demo → NeuroFusion AI presentation platform

## Research Objective

NeuroFusion AI explores how wearable biosignals, behavioral biomarkers, and genetic risk factors can be fused into a personalized NeuroDigital Twin capable of detecting subtle health deviations before clinical symptoms appear.

The project focuses on preventive healthcare, adaptive artificial intelligence, and early risk prediction using data collected from smartwatches, smartphones, and personal health history.
